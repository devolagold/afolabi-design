About Me

My name is
Afolabi Olamilekan

I am a product designer with a foundation in Computer Science, driven by a passion for crafting visually stunning and empowering user experiences.

Self-taught and deeply inspired, my design approach is shaped by a diverse range of influences, balancing creativity with functionality. I prioritize problem-solving and user-centered solutions, ensuring each design is not only aesthetically pleasing but also purposeful and impactful. From concept development to final execution, I combine innovation and practicality to deliver designs that meet the highest standards of excellence. At times, I often try to implement my designs with code.

Outside of work, you’ll often find me exploring fascinating content on YouTube, always eager to learn and be inspired.
